About Cities

Manali

Manali is all about an open valley and with a deodar and pine forest cover, freshwater streams and peaks perennially covered with snow making it a perfect place to unwind and recharge. Not only does it have some fine staying places, but there also many spiritual retreats that call for a visit.

Mumbai

Mumbai (also known as Bombay, the official name until 1995) is the capital city of the Indian state of Maharashtra. Mumbai lies on the Konkan coast on the west coast of India and has a deep natural harbour.
